# Incident Response Policy: Milot Motors
## 1. Purpose
To establish a standard process for detecting, containing, and recovering from security incidents.
## 2. Incident Classification
- **Level 1 (Critical):** Data breach involving Customer PII (Names, VINs).
- **Level 2 (High):** Unauthorized access to the Milot Motors Admin portal.
- **Level 3 (Low):** Spam/Phishing attempts or failed login alerts.
